Continuing their adventure

After dating for a year while they were both finishing at Carleton, Katrina and Will were ready for a big move. They drove across the country starting their 3 year adventure in BC. They spent their free time hiking and exploring the west coast. On one of their weekend trips, Will proposed while they were in Tofino and captured the moment on camera. In November 2016, Katrina accepted a job she couldn't pass up back in Ottawa, so they moved back to the town where they fell in love. They are excited to continue their adventures as husband and wife.
